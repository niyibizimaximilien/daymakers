About Us

Mixing Education with Entertainment

Daymakers Edutainment is a Rwandan comedy and entertainment group that creatively blends education with entertainment. Our mission is to make people laugh while passing along important messages about society, culture, and positive change.

Founded by Clapton Kibonke, the group has become one of Rwanda's most recognized comedy brands, known for our signature show "Bigomba Guhinduka" (which means "things must change").

Through live performances and social media skits, we tackle everyday struggles, habits, and behaviors that need improvement, all while keeping our audience thoroughly entertained.

Our Challenges

Like any growing organization, we've faced internal conflicts, especially over money and contracts. Some members left the group because of disagreements with leadership. But even with these splits, the Daymakers brand has stayed respected in Rwanda.
